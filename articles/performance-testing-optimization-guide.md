## Assessment Process

The assessment process involves evaluating processes, people, tools, and infrastructure. We identify gaps and opportunities for improvement using our green languages to highlight these opportunities.

### Steps

1. **Identify As-Is State**: Understand the current state of performance testing within the organization.
2. **Tailored Approach**: Customize the assessment approach based on the specific needs and constraints of the project.
3. **Deliverables**: Ensure that the assessment results provide value and actionable insights.

## Performance Testing Maturity

Our performance testing maturity model starts with alignment between different stakeholders and progresses through various stages to build a culture of performance testing awareness.

### Stages

1. **Alignment**: Ensure alignment between business and technical teams.
2. **Strategy and Tools**: Create a performance assessment strategy and identify appropriate tools and technologies.
3. **Alerting and Reporting**: Implement alerting mechanisms and results reporting.
