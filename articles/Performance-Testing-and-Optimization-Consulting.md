### Differentiation from Others

Our differentiation lies in our hands-on professionals and product-centric mindset. We focus on making our customers successful in terms of their KPIs and goals. Our support in the performance space and transformation includes building a culture of performance testing awareness.

### When to Seek Consultancy

Consultancy is needed when business owners are unsure about product health, practices, strategies, or approaches to ensure stability post-release. We help answer questions about KPIs, user satisfaction, and defect visibility through gap analysis and assessment.

### Performance Testing Assessment

Our assessment process covers processes, people, toolsets, and infrastructure. We use green languages to highlight opportunities and start by identifying the current state. Each project is unique, so there is no single golden rule for all assessments.

### Performance Testing Maturity

A mature performance testing process starts with alignment between stakeholders, followed by creating a performance assessment strategy and identifying tools and technologies. The goal is to build a culture of performance testing awareness, leading to transformational programs.

### Bottom-Up Approach

We often use a bottom-up approach to start delivering value immediately. This involves identifying typical projects and implementing our target process and architecture. For example, in a project with Prudential Financials, we standardized processes and created intake forms for efficient risk assessments.

### Conclusion

In conclusion, our performance testing and optimization consulting focuses on building a culture of performance testing awareness, aligning stakeholders, and using a bottom-up approach to deliver immediate value. Each project is unique, and our assessments are tailored to meet specific needs.
