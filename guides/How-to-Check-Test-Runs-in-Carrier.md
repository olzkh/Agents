## Overview

This guide provides step-by-step instructions on how to check the performance of your test runs in Carrier. By following these steps, you can easily navigate through the Carrier application and review the performance metrics and results of your tests.

### Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure that you have the following:

- Carrier application installed and set up on your device.
- Access to the Carrier application with the necessary credentials.

### Steps

Follow the steps below to check your test runs in Carrier:

### Step 1: Open Carrier

1. Launch the Carrier application on your device.

### Step 2: Navigate to Performance Tab

1. Once the application is open, locate and click on the 'Performance' tab in the main menu.

### Step 3: Check Overview of Test Runs

1. In the Performance tab, you will find an overview of your test runs. This section provides insights into the performance metrics and results of your tests.

By following these simple steps, you can easily check the performance of your test runs in Carrier and review the necessary metrics and results.
